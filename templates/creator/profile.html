My Artisan Journey

I grew up in the narrow lanes of Varanasi, where the rhythmic sound of handlooms has echoed for centuries. My great-grandfather started our workshop in 1870, and since then, four generations of weavers have kept the tradition alive.

Learning to weave Banarasi silk is not just about mastering technique—it's about understanding the soul of the craft. I spent years as an apprentice under my father, learning to read patterns, mix natural dyes, and feel the quality of silk with my fingertips.

Today, I'm proud to continue this heritage while also adapting to modern tastes. ArtisanAI has helped me reach customers worldwide who appreciate authentic handmade craftsmanship. Every piece I create carries the legacy of my ancestors and the promise of keeping our tradition alive for future generations.
